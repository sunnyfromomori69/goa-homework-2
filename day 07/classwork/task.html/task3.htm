id = "here" About hyperlink

A hyperlink is a digital reference that allows users to navigate from one location to another within a document or to an entirely different document or webpage by clicking or tapping on it. Hyperlinks can be text, images, or buttons, and they are commonly used in web pages to connect to other pages or resources. In HTML, hyperlinks are defined using the tag, which enables users to click and jump to another document or a specific element within a document.

In computing, a hyperlink, or simply a link, is a digital reference to data that the user can follow or be guided to by clicking or tapping. A hyperlink points to a whole document or to a specific element within a document. Hypertext is text with hyperlinks. The text that is linked from is known as anchor text.
